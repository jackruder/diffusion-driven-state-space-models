the report's defaults) consumes

you change the cell set (e.g. add new controls), update that one

* Glossary

- *Cell* — one point of the 18-cell ablation grid, identified by
  =(baseline_form, baseline_mode, tracking_mode)=.  Named e.g.
  =init_mlp_pinned_per_t=.
- *Canonical cell* — =(mlp, pinned, per_t)=, the Phase-C pilot
  baseline.
- *Simplest cell* — =(zero, pinned, fixed)=, mathematically
  equivalent to =DiffusionV2= and locked down by the V2-reduction
  test.
- *Control cell* — Either =init_canonical_ctrl_sigma0= (canonical
  with =sigma_pert=0=) or =init_canonical_ctrl_npretrain0=
  (canonical with =n_pretrain=0=).  Not in the 18-cell grid; not
  Optuna-swept.
- *Handoff* — Stage-1 → stage-2 transition during multi-stage
  training.  Pinned mode freezes μ_p at handoff; Learnable mode
  allows continued updates via the anchor regulariser.
- *σ_data tracking* — =SigmaDataBuffer= records the per-t target
  variance.  Three modes: =fixed= (snapshot at stage-2 start),
  =global_ema= (single EMA pooled across t), =per_t= (independent
  per-timestep EMA).
- *Sweep knobs* — =n_pretrain= + =sigma_pert= + 3 lrs.  Sampled by
  Optuna per trial.  Log-uniform priors; cannot reach zero (hence
  the 2 control cells).
